#📊 Sales Analytics Project – AtliQ Hardwares

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data of AtliQ Hardwares to uncover actionable insights across customers, markets, divisions, and products. 
The analysis focuses on identifying growth trends, performance gaps, and key revenue drivers.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Track customer-wise sales performance and growth 
- Evaluate division-level contribution to overall revenue 
- Analyze market performance against predefined targets 
- Identify top-performing products driving business revenue 

---

## 📂 Reports & Analysis

### 🔹 Customer Performance Report
- Analyzed customer-wise net sales from 2019–2021 
- Identified high-performing customers like Amazon, Atliq Exclusive, and Sage

---

### 🔹 Market Performance vs Target
- Compared actual sales vs target across global markets 
- Identified consistent underperformance across multiple regions 
- Overall market performance was ~9.2% below target 
- Key markets like USA and India showed high sales but still missed targets 

---

### 🔹 Divisional Level Report
- Compared performance across divisions (N & S, P & A, PC) 
- PC division showed highest growth (~313%) 
- P & A contributed highest revenue overall 
- Helped identify which division drives maximum business growth 

---

### 🔹 Top 10 Products Report
- Identified top-performing products contributing major revenue 
- Significant YoY growth observed (some products exceeding 1000% growth) 
- Highlights concentration of revenue among a few key products 

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Revenue is highly concentrated among top customers and products 
- Despite strong sales growth, most markets are underperforming vs targets 
- Certain divisions (like PC) are growing rapidly and can be leveraged further 
- Business has strong expansion potential but requires better target alignment
